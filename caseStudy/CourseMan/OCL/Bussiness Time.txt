
OCL Time constraints: govern govern temporal boundaries for system actions and participant status changes. They establish: 
 1. Defining permissible timeframes for resource disassociation requests;
 2. Linking temporal thresholds to monetary obligations;
 3. Enforcing timely financial fulfillment with consequences;
 4. Automatically adjusting allocations based on payment status;
 5. Flag-or-Deadline (Pay-or-LeadTime): An object is valid if (A) the flag/condition is met (Boolean flag) or (B) the deadline is at least N days away (lead time).
 6. Capping total participation timeframes in the system;
 7. Restricting cumulative temporary withdrawal periods.
